About

Language Archive Cologne

A digital repository for language documentation.

The archive holds primary language data, including audio and video recordings, transcriptions, translations, and annotations.

Dedicated to the long-term preservation and accessibility of these resources for research, education, and cultural heritage.

The archive is sustained by a cross-faculty cooperation at the University of Cologne, bringing together linguistics, research data management, research software engineering, and computing infrastructure.
