About Language Archive Cologne

The Language Archive Cologne (LAC) is a digital archive for language resources. It hosts collections of primary language data, such as audio and video recordings, as well as linguistic analyses, such as transcriptions, translations, and other annotations.

Our mission is to preserve and provide access to language resources for research, education, and cultural heritage purposes. We work with researchers, language communities, and other stakeholders to ensure that language resources are properly documented, archived, and made accessible.
