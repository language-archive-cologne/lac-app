About Language Archive Cologne

The Language Archive Cologne (LAC) is a digital archive of language resources. It contains collections of primary language data, including audio and video recordings, as well as linguistic analyses such as transcriptions, translations and annotations.

Our mission is to preserve these resources and provide access to them for research, educational and cultural heritage purposes. To this end, we collaborate with researchers, language communities, and other stakeholders to ensure that these resources are properly documented, archived, and made accessible.
