The Data User Agreement outlines terms for researchers accessing CLARIN data through the repository.

Permitted Use

The User agrees to use the CLARIN data only for the research purposes specified in this DUA and the attached Application.

Confidentiality Requirements

Users must protect data identifying individuals and maintain secure access, restricting visibility to named researchers only.

Non-Transferability

The agreement cannot be shared with unauthorized parties without written approval from the Content Provider.

Research Reporting

All findings must exclude personal identifiers when disclosed publicly.

Data Status

The archive provides materials "as is" without performance guarantees or liability for data quality issues.

Duration

The agreement lasts two years from acceptance or until either party provides written termination notice.

Third-Party Access

Anyone receiving substantial content portions must cite the original source per CLARIN standards.

Jurisdiction

German law governs this agreement. Commercial applications of the archived linguistic resources are prohibited.
