This privacy policy governs data collection and usage for the Language Archive Cologne repository, operated by the Data Center for The Humanities (DCH) in Cologne as a CLARIN-Service Provider.

Personal Data Collection

Users may be required to register with personal information including name, email address, username and password. Automatic data collection includes IP addresses, access timestamps, and visited pages.

Data Usage

We use personal data to provide requested CLARIN services, handle communications, conduct research analysis, and maintain system security.

Data Sharing

Personal data is not sold for marketing purposes. However, limited identification data and access rights are shared with other CLARIN-Service Providers to enable cross-platform access.

User Rights

Users can request copies of information we hold about them and request corrections to any inaccuracies.

Security and Storage

Only authorized staff have access to the data. Data is retained only as long as necessary for stated purposes.

Governing Law

This policy is subject to German law under the jurisdiction of Cologne courts.
