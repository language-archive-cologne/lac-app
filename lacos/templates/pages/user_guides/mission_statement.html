The Language Archive Cologne (LAC) exists to support research, education, and teaching through high-quality digital language resources.

Core Functions

  • Preserving digital audio, video, annotations, and language data long-term
  • Facilitating open online access to research materials
  • Promoting sustainable practices for digital language data in humanities

Institutional Role

The LAC operates as part of the Data Center for the Humanities (DCH) at the University of Cologne's Faculty of Arts and Humanities, which manages diverse digital data preservation and accessibility.

CLARIN-D Integration

As a CLARIN-D resource centre repository, the LAC serves the broader mission to provide "linguistic data, tools and services in an integrated, interoperable and scalable infrastructure" for social sciences and humanities research.

The archive is committed to open access, technical support for researchers, and long-term digital stewardship within an integrated research infrastructure network.
