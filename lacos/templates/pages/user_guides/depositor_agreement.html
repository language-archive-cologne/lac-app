Depositors must establish specific deposition license agreements with CLARIN centres hosting their resources. In the deposition agreement, the depositor also specifies the license(s) with which the resource will be distributed to end-users.

Model Agreements

There are three minimal model CLARIN deposition license agreements:

  • CLARIN-DELA-PUB-v1.0 - For publicly available resources
  • CLARIN-DELA-ACA-v1.0 - For academic-use resources
  • CLARIN-DELA-RES-v1.0 - For restricted-access resources

Selection Tool

The CLARIN License Category Calculator helps depositors identify appropriate licensing based on resource content and intended uses.

Additional Details

  • Access conditions vary by resource category
  • Centers and depositors may negotiate additional specific terms beyond minimal requirements
  • The model agreements function as checklists for compliance
